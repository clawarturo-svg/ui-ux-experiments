# Reference: dark-editorial-rain

## Source
Derived from a reference shared by Sebastian on 2026-03-15 and the first landing implementation in this repo.

## Why it matters
This style is a strong reusable base for premium product pages.

## Key signals
- oversized hero typography
- dark luxury atmosphere
- subtle animated background
- rounded glass cards
- restrained accent colors
- minimal but elegant navigation

## Best suited for
- AI tools
- software products
- design/creative studios
- technical thought leadership pages

## Avoid when
- the product needs warm/friendly playful energy
- the page needs heavy documentation density
- the target aesthetic is corporate and conservative
